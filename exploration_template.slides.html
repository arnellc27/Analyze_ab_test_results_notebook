Prosper Loan Data¶

by Chris Arnell¶

Prosper is an online loan company based in San Francisco. They do not have a brick and mortar store like most banks, and this means Prosper has a much lower cost of business than a normal bank. The data supplied contains information for Loans granted between 2009 & 2014

What is the structure of your dataset?¶

The Prosper Loans Data contains 113937 entries for loans given by Prosper after July 2009. Each entry has 81 variables, this seemed rather excessive so using the variable dictionary supplied by Udacity I highlighted variables that were of interest to me...

What is/are the main feature(s) of interest in your dataset?¶

The main features I am going to concentrate my exploration around are:

  • ListingCreationDate, Term, BorrowerAPR, BorrowerRate, EstimatedEffectiveYield, EstimatedLoss, EstimatedReturn, ProsperScore, CreditScoreRangeLower0, CreditScoreRangeUpper, Occupation, StatedMonthlyIncome & LoanOriginalAmount'

What features in the dataset do you think will help support your investigation into your feature(s) of interest?¶

I will focus are the Borrower rate, Eastimated return, Year, Credit Score and also the Prosper Score
